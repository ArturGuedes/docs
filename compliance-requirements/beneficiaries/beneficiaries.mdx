---
title: "Beneficiaries"
---

Beneficiaries are Individuals or Businesses that will send or receive payments individuals or businesses that will send or receive payments using fiat and stablecoins.

For compliance purposes, every beneficiary is also considered the originator of the funds. This means that when creating a beneficiary and initiating a payment, the beneficiary will be regarded as the source of the fiat currency or stablecoin.

By registering your users with Lumx through the Customers API, you can enable seamless transfers of stablecoins or fiat currencies from or to your users' wallets or bank accounts. All KYC and KYB checks are handled by Walapay so you can safely move funds, knowing that Walapay has properly vetted your users in compliance with legal requirements.
